# Supply Chain Operations Policy

## Delivery Risk Policy
Any shipment taking more than 5 days should be flagged as delayed. Delayed shipments should be reviewed by the operations team.

## Revenue Risk Policy
Revenue at risk is calculated when delayed shipments are connected to active customer orders. High-value delayed orders should be prioritized.

## Regional Performance Policy
Regions with lower revenue or higher delay rates should be reviewed for operational bottlenecks.

## Product Performance Policy
Products with high revenue and frequent delays should be monitored closely because they affect customer satisfaction and business performance.

## Escalation Policy
If a shipment is delayed and revenue at risk is high, the issue should be escalated to operations leadership.
